About the Film

Groupchat is a psychological horror film that blurs the lines between reality and online gaming. After months of absence, Games Master Dean returns with a new campaign, but the game takes a dark turn. What started as a way to reconnect with friends becomes a chilling fight for survival.

Director's Vision

Director Dan Brownlie crafted Groupchat as a deeply personal project. Inspired by his own experiences with grief, the film explores themes of loss, isolation, and the dangers lurking in the digital world.

Production Insights

The film was shot over four intense days using a unique real-time approach. The actors performed live over separate video calls, ensuring an authentic sense of tension and unpredictability. The use of practical effects and immersive storytelling techniques made this project a groundbreaking take on the screen-life horror genre.
