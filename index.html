New role playing game coming soon

Step Into the Forgotten World with Necromancer.

Introducing Necromancer, the groundbreaking RPG from Mean Productions and Brand-B Games. Set in a time before humanity drove mystical creatures to extinction, Necromancer offers a fresh, immersive take on fantasy role-playing.

Here, fantasy meets reality — every creature you encounter once roamed our world. Play as an authentic Ogre, Goblin, or other ancient entities, and harness the power of spells and magic long suppressed by modern society. Ever wonder what it was like to live in a world where myths and monsters shaped everyday life? Now's your chance to find out.

Built on extensive research into historical myths and folklore, Necromancer delivers a richly detailed world that blurs the line between fantasy and reality. It's not just a game — it's a journey into an era lost to legend.

So, gather your group, sharpen your strategy, and get ready to roll the dice. A new era of immersive role-playing awaits.
