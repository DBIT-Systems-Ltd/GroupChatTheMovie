Monsters & Entities That Await You

The Melting Man

A horrifying figure of liquefying flesh and oozing misery, the Melting Man was once human but has been twisted by a curse or exposure to a forbidden alchemical experiment. His skin drips like wax, pooling into acidic sludge that corrodes anything it touches. Despite his disfigured appearance, his mind is still intact, filled with bitterness and a desire for revenge on those responsible for his condition.

Bone Whisperer

o A spectral entity that manipulates the bones of the dead. It commands skeletal creatures and can merge bones to form horrific constructs.
o Abilities: Bone manipulation, skeletal armour, summon skeletons
o Hook: It guards a lost crypt of powerful necromantic relics.

Hollow Husk

o A walking corpse filled with void energy. Its hollow eyes emit a dark, pulsing aura that weakens living beings.
o Abilities: Life drain aura, void scream, invulnerability to non-magical weapons
o Hook: Created by a necromancer as an accidental side effect of their experiments.

Wailing Revenant

o A tormented spirit seeking vengeance. Its mournful wail paralyses victims with fear.
o Abilities: Paralysis shriek, possession, ethereal movement
o Hook: Its presence is tied to a cursed battlefield where its entire family was slain.

The Withered Knight

o A former champion who fell in battle and was revived to serve a dark master. Its body is wrapped in ancient, enchanted bandages.
o Abilities: Immense strength, decay touch, weapon mastery
o Hook: Seeks redemption but is bound by necromantic seals.
